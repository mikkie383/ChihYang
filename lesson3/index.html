Site Name: lunteer

This website will not only provide information about the local weather, it will also offer volunteer opportunity and some local business discount information. The name, WEAlunteer, comes from weather and volunteer.

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to let people get involved with and improve their community.

While people can gain and learn some real world experience, businessmen will give good deals in return. Or people can simply go on the site and check what discount local businesses have for that day. It will also help to increase the awareness of the local businesses.
