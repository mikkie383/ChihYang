Become a Member

There are many benefits of being a part of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce. Depends on your business types and purposes, we will provide different services. The price range is from $100 to $1000.

Non-profit ($100)

If you ran a non-profit business, this is the option. You pay the annual fee, $100. This allows you to:

  • Get discount from the partnership/membership stores
  • Get free counciling twice
  • Receive 10% off for the advertisement
  • Free access to the business training twice

Bronze ($200)

Try to start a business? This is the right choice for you. With $200 annual fee, you will be able to receive what Non-profit membership has. Also, you will:

  • Receive free business training 3 time a year
  • Get a business evaluation once every 6 month
  • Obtain 20% off for advertisement

Silver ($400)

For those who already have their businesses, but you are looking for some improvement. This is a great option. $400 annual fee, you can have what Bronze membership has. On top of that, you can:

  • Receive free business training 4 time a year
  • Attand Utah Business Conference for free
  • Free monthly advertisement

Gold ($1000)

If you are looking for an exponential growth, Gold membership is for you. Annual fee is $1000, you will receive what's above and you can:

  • Obtain business training and counciling unlimited time
  • Receive an business evaluation every month
  • 80% off for partnership pairing
