What is WEAlunteer?

We are a volunteer organization located in Rexburg, Idaho. Our goal is to improve communities, particularly in these three towns (Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven) in Idaho. We provide the local weather data for the locals and people who pass through. Mostly importantly, we offer people oppertunities to serve. We collaborate with many local businesses. Every week, we will have different events to help to build a better community. Those events will be run by volunteers. If you are looking to serve or interested in certain oppertunities. Please contact us.
